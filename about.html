About Cashlytics

Your Complete Financial Management Solution

Cashlytics is a comprehensive personal finance management platform that I built as part of my 5th Semester MPR project. What started as a simple expense tracker has evolved into a powerful financial portfolio manager that helps users effectively manage their entire financial life.

Built With Modern Technologies
HTML5 CSS3 JavaScript Bootstrap IndexedDB

Key Capabilities

Everything you need for complete financial control

Account Management

Manage multiple bank accounts, credit cards, loans, and cash in one place.

Investment Tracking

Monitor stocks, mutual funds, SIPs with automatic profit/loss calculations.

EMI Management

Track all loans and EMIs with automated calculations and projections.

Tax-Saving Schemes

Manage PPF, FD, RD, and other investment schemes with maturity tracking.

Visual Analytics

Get insights with charts, graphs, and comprehensive financial reports.

100% Privacy

All data stored locally on your device. No cloud, no servers, complete security.
